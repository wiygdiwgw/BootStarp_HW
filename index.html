Searching for a fun, affordable way to pay tribute to some of the most iconic Star Wars™ characters and moments? These are the sets you’re looking for. Introduce young Star Wars™ fans to a galaxy far, far away or recreate scenes from your favourite fictional universe with our collection of small LEGO® Star Wars™ sets. Enjoy all the fun of Star Wars™, without committing to a big build! Whether you're a Padawan learner or a fully-fledged Jedi Master, adventure awaits with these small LEGO® Star Wars™ building toys.

The Star Wars™ galaxy is full of iconic characters, legendary scenes and memorable locales. If your kids are just getting to know the Star Wars™ galaxy, finding an engaging gift can be tricky. We’ve handpicked some cool and unique holiday and birthday gifts for kids.
